About Me


My name is Erik Alfvin and I live in Saint Paul, MN. I am originally from Wisconsin and unfortunately still a Packer fan. I currently work for the State of Minnesota in the Department of Commerce, Weights & Measures Division. We carry out precision mass measurements on items used for research purposes and to ensure that commercial scales are accurate throughout the state.

Outside of work I enjoy nordic skiing and developing software for personal projects. In the future, I would like to develop software in a scientific or engineering setting full time.
