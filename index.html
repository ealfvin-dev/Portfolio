PyMac Data Reduction Software:
Data reduction tool for precision mass metrology measurements. Finds the least squares best fit matrix solution for a given laboratory measurement design.


Weather Dashboard:
A tool to get the current and future weather for a given location using the OpenWeather API. Keeps track of a user's recent searches.





Satellite Orbit Simulator:
A simulation for visualizing satellite orbits around the earth at different altitudes and velocities. Uses Newtonian mechanics to calculate gravitational force and acceleration.

Virtual art gallery:
Build your own virtual art galleries from items from the vast collection of artwork in the Metropolitan Museum of Art API.
